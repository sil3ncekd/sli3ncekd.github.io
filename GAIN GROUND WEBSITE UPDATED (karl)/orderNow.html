How to Order?

1. Choose your items

On our website, you will see different types of food that we offer. From our sweestest ensaymadas up to our heavy meals that will be worth every penny you spend.

2. List your orders

On your notepad or notes, right down the food name you like! We love to bake them all for you.

3. Click the "Call Us Now" button

To proceed with your order click the "Call Us Now" button below. It will prompt you to your Messenger in order for you to chat with one of our admins.

4. Chat with one of the admin

By chatting with one of our admins, you can ask for questions especially the payment method or the delivery process thoroughly. This will be also the final step in order to order our food.

5. Book a delivery rider

Finally by chatting with our admins, you may choose the delivery options that will depend on what the customer and the company has talked about.

Order --> Prepare --> Pack --> Deliver
