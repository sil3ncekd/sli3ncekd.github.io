About Us

The family business started in 2017, it had the small goal to provide delicious and filling food to the owners circle of friends, The business then started selling those products to customers on different social medias and gradually became a full fledged business delivering its products around the local vicinity. Kookaye’s D’lite’s name came from one of the owners first name, Kaye. We as business owners, and as a family, want to provide the happiness and fulfillment that food can bring to our family, to yours.

Our History

As business owners, and as the ones who create the products, we want to give the best to our consumers, using the best ingredients that can be found in the market, giving time and quality to each and every detail and most importantly the love of making food to create the best experience for each consumer that we cater to. We endeavor to go to this extent to cater to people who know and appreciate good food, In the food industry, we are used to seeing dull and familiar products that often times we ignore and just buy it because we are hungry, but for us, eating food should be an experience that takes you on to have a greater appreciation to not only the food, but to who and how it was made.

Despite the challenges, We have found that being a business owner is incredibly rewarding. We am able to create and build something that is mine, and We have the freedom to make decisions and take the business in the direction that We want. We also enjoy the sense of accomplishment that comes from seeing our business grow and thrive.

Overall, being business owners have been an exciting and fulfilling experience, and we are so grateful for the opportunities it has given to our family.
